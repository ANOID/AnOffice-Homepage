Plans and Pricing

Pay As You Use - $15/day

From coworking to conference rooms, if you want to just pay for what you use with no regular monthly fee, this is the way to go.

  • Access on a per day basis, M-F, 8am - 6pm
  • High speed WiFi
  • Free gated parking
  • Mini-fridge and microwave use
  • Unlimited complimentary coffee + tea
  • 2 pages/day of printing
  • Access to the office community and network

Conference room reservations - $25/4 hr meeting

  • Must be signed up with valid credit card on file in order to submit request
  • Allow at least 2 business days notice for your request
  • Reservations subject to availability

Office Regular - $40/mo

Planning to get a lot of work done (and maybe drinking a lot of free coffee)? This is the membership for you.

  • Unlimited access M-F: 6am-10pm, Sat: 8am-5pm
  • High speed WiFi
  • Free gated parking
  • One complimentary conference room reservation per month
  • Additional conference room reservations $15/4 hrs
  • Access to over half a dozen Detroit coworking spaces with the Co-Lab Detroit Coworking Pass
  • Mini-fridge and microwave use
  • Unlimited complimentary coffee + tea
  • 5 pages/week of printing
  • Access to the office community and network
  • Satisfaction of knowing you’re helping support this kind of awesome place in the city

Cancel at any time with no added fees.

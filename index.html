An Office in Detroit is a shared workspace.
It's a place where you can go to do some work.
It's your office... and other people's, too.


For example, we don't have a place for you to work on building a transformer (mostly cause it sounds like it would be especially dirty and noisy). You could work on the plans for the transformer, and then have a meeting here to raise funding to build your transformer, or maybe film a kickstarter video outside so you can raise the money. But then, when you have the money, you'll probably need to find another workspace for the actual building and testing phase. We hope you'll still come to visit, though, and invite us down to see your frickin' awesome transformer!
